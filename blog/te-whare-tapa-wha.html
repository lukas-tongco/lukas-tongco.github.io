Wellbeing

Physical

To strengthen my wall of physical wellbeing, I play badminton at least once a week in order to stay active. I also try my best to go sleep and wake up at the same time consistently, and to eat 3-4 meals a day.

Family

To strengthen my wall of family wellbeing, I ensure that I eat my meals at the same time as my family. When there's a chance, I video-call my extended family who lives overseas in order to stay in touch with them. I also like to go hang out with my friends.

Emotional & Mental

To strengthen my wall of emotional and mental wellbeing, I like to play badminton as it is my favorite sport which makes me happy and relieves stress. Going out to eat my favourite foods that I usually don't have is also something I enjoy.

Spiritual

To strengthen my wall of spiritual wellbeing, I like to stick to my beliefs and values instead of blindly following things without purpose, which helps set my own identity.

Foundation

To strengthen my connection to my environment, I like to quietly take in what's happening around my backyard. Many birds such as Tūī often visit, and they all have their unique calls and behaviours. It's really interesting to listen and watch while they fly around, look for food, or just sit there.
