Home
  • Emotional Intelligence is the ability to identify and manage your own emotions and the emotions of others.

  • EQ is different to IQ because EQ refers to one’s emotional ability while IQ refers to one’s mental ability. EQ is how well you perceive and express emotions while IQ is a calculated score related to things like your knowledge of the world.

  • EQ is important because it can make you happier, manage stress better, and work more efficiently in teams among other things.
